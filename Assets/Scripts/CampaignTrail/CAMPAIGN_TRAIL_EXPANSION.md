### The Nemesis Reporter

A persistent reporter character who:
- **Tracks you** across events
- **Investigates** specific angles
- **Has evidence** they claim to have
- **Has sources** (Deep Throat style)
- **Ambushes you** at the worst moments
- **Builds relationship** over time (nemesis or respect)

### Investigation Angles

1. **Campaign Finance** - FEC filings, shell companies, donations
2. **Personal History** - Photographs, witnesses, past behavior
3. **Policy Flip-Flops** - Voting record contradictions
4. **Staff Treatment** - Resignations, hostile work environment
5. **Donor Relationships** - Quid pro quo allegations
6. **Family Business** - Conflict of interest claims

### Full Ambush Scenario

