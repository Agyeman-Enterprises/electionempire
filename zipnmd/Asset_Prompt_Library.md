## STYLE FOUNDATION

### Master Style Prompt (Add to ALL generations)

```
STYLE SUFFIX (append to every prompt):
", editorial political cartoon style, satirical caricature, 
exaggerated features, bold ink outlines, New Yorker magazine aesthetic, 
professional illustration, slightly grotesque humor, 
high contrast, clean composition --ar [RATIO] --style raw"

NEGATIVE PROMPT (use where supported):
"--no photorealistic, 3D render, anime, manga, childish, 
blurry, low quality, watermark, signature"
```

### Aspect Ratios
- **Portraits:** --ar 3:4 (vertical)
- **Busts/Head shots:** --ar 1:1 (square)
- **Locations/Scenes:** --ar 16:9 (wide)
- **UI Elements:** --ar 1:1 (square)
- **Vertical Banners:** --ar 9:16
